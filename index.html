RESEARCH STATEMENT

Currently, my research focuses on three areas: cross-spectral imaging, machine learning and robotics. The primary objective of my work is to find valuable relationships between images from different spectra or modalities, which can make easier to solve problems that currently are difficult or impossible to solve just using information from just one spectrum. Also, I am interested in robotics and industrial applications that involve the use of computer vision.
